Scientific Arguments for CPC Theory

  1. Universal Expansion via Internal Pressure: Galaxies are receding due to cosmic pressure, not just gravity (Hubble Redshift).
  2. Structural Stability: Spiral galaxies resist collapse, indicating containment pressure in effect.
  3. Black Holes & Neutron Stars: Extreme gravity is confined, suggesting a counteracting containment force.
  4. Uniform CMBR: Background radiation uniformity indicates widespread pressure equilibrium.
  5. Matter-Antimatter Asymmetry: Imbalance may result from containment mechanisms post-Big Bang.
  6. Quantum Vacuum Pressure: Casimir effect and vacuum energy imply existent pressure in “empty” space.
  7. Gravity's Shortcomings: Can't unify quantum-gravity or explain accelerated expansion fully.
  8. Edge Constraints: Cosmic containment field hypothesis explains universal boundary behavior.
  9. Architectural Analogy: Cosmic structure resembles tension-compression systems in engineering.
  10. Biological Pressure Systems: Earthly systems (blood pressure, hydrostatic pressures) mirror cosmic processes.
